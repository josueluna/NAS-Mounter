Features

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

NAS-Mountie is built around one principle: your NAS should be available when you need it, without you having to think about it.

Silent auto-mount

Reconnects your saved shares automatically when your Mac wakes on a known network — no clicks, no windows, no interruptions.

Network profiles

Saves a separate NAS profile per Wi-Fi network. Home, office, studio — each network remembers its own shares and credentials.

Keychain security

Credentials are stored in the macOS Keychain — never in plain text, never in app files. Your password stays on your Mac.

Menu bar native

Lives quietly in your menu bar. A single click opens the popover — no Dock icon required, no app switching needed.

Multi-share mounting

Browse all available SMB shares from your NAS, select one or several, and mount them in a single action.

Launch at login

Starts automatically with your Mac. By the time you sit down, your shares are already mounted and ready.

How it works

Set up once.
Works forever.

01

Enter your NAS details

Type your NAS IP address, username, and password in the popover.

02

Browse and select shares

NAS-Mountie discovers all available SMB shares. Pick one or several.

03

Mount and save

Click Mount. Enable Remember Password to save a profile for this network.

04

Done — forever

Next time you're on this network, shares mount automatically and silently.
